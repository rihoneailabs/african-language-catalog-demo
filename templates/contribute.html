How You Can Help

The African Languages Catalog thrives on community contributions. Your knowledge and expertise can help us create a more comprehensive and accurate resource. Here are some ways you can contribute:

  • Add information about a language not yet in our catalog
  • Suggest updates or corrections to existing language entries
  • Provide audio recordings of languages you speak
  • Share cultural context or interesting facts about a language
  • Help translate our website into more languages

Submission Form

What Happens Next?

After you submit your contribution:

  1. Our team will review your submission for accuracy and relevance.
  2. We may reach out to you for additional information or clarification if needed.
  3. If approved, your contribution will be added to the catalog, and you'll be credited for your help (unless you prefer to remain anonymous).
  4. We'll notify you by email when your contribution has been published.
