Linguistic Diversity

Africa boasts over 1,500 languages across major families, reflecting unparalleled diversity.

  • Countries like Nigeria host hundreds of languages.
  • Many languages are endangered with few speakers.
  • Languages like Swahili and Hausa have millions of speakers.
